Q) What is a Chassis Number? Where can I find it?

A) The Chassis Number is a unique serial number to identify your Royal Enfield motorcycle. Please refer to your Owner’s Manual to find the location of your Chassis Number.

  

Q) What is the process to add a motorcycle in my profile?

A) Please log in to the Royal Enfield app with your registered mobile number/email address. Please enter either your motorcycle registration number or the Chassis Number. If your mobile number matches with the one you provided at the time of the purchase of the motorcycle, it will automatically get added to your profile on the app.

 

Q) What if my mobile number is different from the one registered at the time of motorcycle purchase?

A) If your mobile number does not match our records, you will have to upload the current RC copy of your motorcycle along with any Government of India approved identification proof. Upon verification of your documents, your motorcycle will get linked to your profile on the app. 

 

Q) I'm unable to book a service from the app.

A) Please close the app & launch it again. Sometimes, there might be some server or network issues which may take some time to be resolved.

 

Q) Can I make a payment via the app?

A) You can pay for your motorcycle service directly through the app. You can also book your motorcycle and pay the booking amount through the app. You can also complete the payment for your motorcycle through the app.

 

Q) How to change my password?

A) Go to Profile screen in the app, then go to settings page and click on "Change password" link.

 

Q) How do I check the status of the service booked? 

A) You can track the status in the Service screen also you will get notifications on the service progress. 

 

Q) Once I booked the service, when do I go and drop the motorcycle?

A) Please reach the designated Service Centre at least 15 minutes before the scheduled time.

 

Q) Is Pick-up and Drop available as part of the service?

A) Currently, Pick-up and Drop facility is not available to be booked through the app. Coming soon though.
